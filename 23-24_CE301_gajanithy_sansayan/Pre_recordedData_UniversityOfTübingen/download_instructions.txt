As the files are of large size, the data are suggested to be downloaded wisely. 
The following Pre_recorded data and their size are listed below:
Mango ---------> 2.7 GB
Kiwi ----------> 44 GB
Papaya --------> 22 GB
Avocado -------> 72 GB

To download this data follow this process on your Linux Mechine.

1. Download or copy the desired shell script of the desired data of the fruit. 
2. Make the script executable by running the following command:
                 chmod +x SHELL_SCRIPT_NAME
3. Run the scipt:
                 ./SHELL_SCRIPT_NAME
